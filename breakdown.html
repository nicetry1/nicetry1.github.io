next steps

interviews

The next step in the production process will be recording interviews in the Bay Area, which we hope to do in the Summer of 2023 pending funding to compensate collaborators for their time and expertise. Intended interview participants include the Niles Film Museum's resident historian, representatives from the Association of Ramaytush Ohlone, a Bay Area based geologist, and founders of the Western Neighborhoods Project.

archival research

While we have already embarked on extensive research for the project, the next steps will be to assess the body of material gathered so far in concert with the stories and information learned in the interviews to refine and focus research efforts. Grant funding would help us to fund access to institutional archives, digitization of archival materials, and copyright clearances..

post production

The final step will be post production, sifting through over 100 hours of footage and field recordings and a large body of research to create a linear portrait of the material. Grant funding will enable us to cover post production costs in editing, sound design, and scoring.
