next steps

development

The next step in the process for us is technical and creative development of the interactive game interface for the documentary. Noa and Sadie will work together to transfer the hundreds of hours of media, from interviews to footage from the site to archival materials, into an interactive map in which the player can navigate many strands of this place and its complex histories. The team will also be applying for grant funding during this time to support the degree of labor involved in creating an interactive project like this.

archival research

While we have already embarked on extensive research for the project, the next steps will be to assess the body of material gathered so far in concert with learnings from the interview process to refine and focus research efforts. Grants will help fund in-person access to institutional archives, high-quality digitization of archival materials, and copyright clearances.
